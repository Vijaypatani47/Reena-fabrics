Who We Are

Crafted in Surat, Worn Worldwide

Reenam Fabrics Private Limited is a premier manufacturer of high-end embroidered net and lace fabrics, rooted in the heart of India's textile capital — Surat, Gujarat.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses advanced MVD73 multi-drive embroidery machines alongside a fully equipped design studio, ensuring every metre of fabric meets the highest global standards.

From bridal couture to ready-to-wear fashion, our collections blend traditional craftsmanship with modern precision.
